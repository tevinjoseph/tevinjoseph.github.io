Love 24*7 - Life repeats - Review

Sun 19 July 2015

Image courtesy: www.india.com

Love 24*7 is Sreebala K Menon's debut directorial venture starring Dileep and newcomer Nikhila. Film portrays a story which happens in a news channel 'Nalamidam'.

Roopesh Nambiar(Dileep) is the channel's leading news presenter and Kabani (Nikhila) is a trainee who joins the channel. They fell in love as the film progresses.

The acting of Dileep and Nikhila are the highlights of the cinema. Both of them delivered an impressive performance. Nikhila has done her role sans any debutante's nerve.

The director is able to narrate the love story well and the movie goes steadily with some light-hearted comedies.

Sreenivasan came up with one of the best performances of him recently as Chief editor. Lena also delivers a strong performance.

There is a subplot between Suhasini and Sasikumar. Both of them are doctors and this plot let the film down. Their story is a bit slow and sometimes boring.

The climax is another low point of the movie. Apart from these two issues, the film is a good one to watch.

Love 24*7 is not an entertainer but if one is ready to watch a feel good movie, this one is for you. For Dileep fans, this is a movie to remember because their hero is back to choosing his films well.

Rating : 2.75/5
